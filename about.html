Short Biography

I’m Adam, a 22 year old Experience Design student currently residing in Kil, Sweden. I previously studied Computer Networks and Communcation Technology in High School before deciding to make the switch to a more design focused area due to my growing interest.


From my education in Experience Design I’ve grown to be proficient in UX/UI & Interactive design, while also learning and getting alot of experience in other areas such as User Research & Gameification.


I’ve enjoyed creating and solving problems in varying forms ever since I was a child, although the challenges have changed from simple lego constructions and games to culinary experiences and digital products.
